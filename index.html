Can foreigners own property in Indonesia?
Yes. Through established legal structures (Right to Use / PT PMA), foreign investors can securely own property in Indonesia. We handle all legal setup for you with trusted local attorneys.
What's the construction timeline?
Construction begins in 2026, with key handover expected late 2027 to early 2028 (12‚Äì18 months build time). You'll receive regular progress updates with photos and video throughout every phase. Once you place your 30% deposit, you'll be added to our exclusive Yara Estates WhatsApp Community for real-time updates.
How does the rental income work?
We offer optional property management that handles everything ‚Äî listing, guest management, cleaning, and maintenance. Owners typically see 12‚Äì18% annual yields from short-term rental platforms like Airbnb.
Is Lombok safe and accessible?
Absolutely. Lombok has direct international flights, modern infrastructure (especially around Mandalika), excellent healthcare facilities, and is consistently rated as one of the safest destinations in Southeast Asia.
What if I want to visit my villa?
It's your villa! Use it whenever you want. Many owners block out personal weeks and rent the rest of the year. Our management team ensures it's always ready for your arrival.

We offer flexible payment plans tied to construction milestones: 30% on reservation & design, 30% at foundation & structure, 30% at finishing & furnishing, and a final 10% at key handover. See our transparent pricing section for full details.

Exclusivity is core to our philosophy. A small, premium community ensures high property values, personalised service, and a genuine sense of privacy and belonging for every owner.
